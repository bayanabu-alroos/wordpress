Lightweightly Blog is a minimal blog theme made for writers and bloggers who love traveling and adventures. The design is flexible so you can use it to create your own newspaper where you write about your hobbies. Change header image and write about food, fashion or whatever you like! We are aware that affiliate, AdSense and other monetization options are important for the business aspect of being a writer, the theme has a lot of space for that, both in the widgets and content. We have made Lightweightly Blog fast, lightweight and SEO optimized (Search Engine Optimization) so you can outrank your competitors on Google. We are fully aware of how important it is to have an SEO friendly, we will keep updating the theme.

## Description

Lightweightly Blog is a minimal blog theme made for writers and bloggers who love traveling and adventures. The design is flexible so you can use it to create your own newspaper where you write about your hobbies. Change header image and write about food, fashion or whatever you like! We are aware that affiliate, AdSense and other monetization options are important for the business aspect of being a writer, the theme has a lot of space for that, both in the widgets and content. We have made Lightweightly Blog fast, lightweight and SEO optimized (Search Engine Optimization) so you can outrank your competitors on Google. We are fully aware of how important it is to have an SEO friendly, we will keep updating the theme.
